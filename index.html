HOME-OWNERSHIP RATE

Home-ownership rate is the percentage of homes that owned by the occupants in US. The data for home-ownership is generated by the US Census Bureau.
Having a house ownership usually indicates one's stability in economy and social status. Therefore, understand the homeownship rate in a contry is very important. This measure can dirtectly reflect/influence the social and economic behaivor in the contry. In here, we will explore the homeownship rate in different age groups and different marital status.
